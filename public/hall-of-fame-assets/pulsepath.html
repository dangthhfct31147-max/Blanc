Project Brief

Problem

Nha truong thuong nhan ra hoc sinh qua tai tam ly qua muon vi khong co check-in deu va khong biet uu tien follow-up truong hop nao truoc.

Solution

Check-in 60 giay moi tuan, triage rule-based va queue can thiep cho co van hoc duong voi lop safeguard ro rang.

Impact

74% check-in dung lich • 27 case duoc xu ly trong 48 gio dau • quy trinh de gap co van ro hon
